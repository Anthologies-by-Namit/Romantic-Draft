Harmony's Quest: Two Halves - Romantic Draft Chapter 2

The dream of uniting two halves
seems quite difficult to be caught off guard,
though I'm aware it's perfectly aligned;
Us to get unite.

If I'm the dark moon at night,
I need her rays of the sun every second of time
to brighten this obscure night.

If she's the bright moon of this dark night,
I intend to be that one star
of this starless night.
Together we can beautify this spooky night.

If she's the magician,
I intend to be the wand.
If she casts the spells,
I intend to be the first word of her.
It's more like we are
enchanted by the spells of Held-together.

If she's a writer,
I intend to be a paper,
so our relationship can be written forever.

If she's a waterfall,
I intend to be a mountain.
Together we can create such a gorgeous pattern.

If she likes to roam around,
I intend to be a big greenery ground,
so I can work as oxygen in this hazardous taint that is around.

If she likes the shooting stars,
I intend to drag them at the beautiful hands of her.
By the single touch of her,
stars found to gain consciousness as the nightly moonflower.

If she likes to sing,
I intend to be the instrument of her every music,
so the rhythmic
of ours can be live as the legacies.

If she likes something,
I intend to be the part of that something,
so it might bring a little giggling.
I always try to be the reason for her smile,
so I won't be a bunch of regrets at the end of life.

Us is like a missing piece
of our puzzling grace,
and I'm pursuing that missing piece
as my heart pace.
It's more like we're
the two points on a page
who can form a well-defined line.
Once we're linked, our destinies align.

Us is like the two halves of a single line,
which is waiting to unite.
Her one smile can fix the void of mine,
while if I find her this time.

I'm on my way to seek for the
smile of her and mine,
so we can enjoy this time...

~Namit Agarwal
