The Forbidden Tale

The tale before fortnight
wandering for quiet time;
Mind wasn't allowed to recollect
as heart ever docile.

The tale was forbidden
to even give a thought;
The ill-fated work of pen
wielded by a writer's distraught.

The tale was regarded
tragedy of two lives;
Can't help in the world of disguised
distinct by fallacious beliefs.

~Namit Agarwal
